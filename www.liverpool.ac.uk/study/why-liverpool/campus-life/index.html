Campus life

Our beautiful campus offers the perfect environment to learn and live in. With everything you need close at hand, and fantastic facilities and services to support you, you'll be right at home.

Sport Liverpool

Whatever your sporting interests, we have a dedicated and growing team of professional staff to help you get the best out of our excellent facilities.

Liverpool Guild of Students

As soon as you join the University of Liverpool, you automatically become a member of the Guild of Students and from your first day as a student and throughout your university years, the Guild will make sure you enjoy your time at Liverpool.

The University libraries

With two University libraries on campus, the Sydney Jones library and the Harold Cohen library, you will have 24/7 access to books, computers, study spaces and library support. Depending on your study preferences, the library provides zoned workspaces for silent, quiet and group study. We will encourage you to excel in your studies with KnowHow, a Library service that offers face-to-face and online support sessions and resources to help you.

Spaces on campus

Whether it's a space to take a walk outside of your studies, or meet up with friends, our campus is full of green spaces where you can take some air. You'll also be spoilt for choice for cafes, bars and restaurants on campus and nearby, from the independents clustered in places like Hope Street and Faulkner Street, to all the main coffee chains dotted about.
