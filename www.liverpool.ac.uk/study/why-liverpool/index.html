Why choose Liverpool?

It’s an important question. And there are thousands of answers. There’s our global outlook for a start, and our spirit of originality. Our warm and welcoming environment and the incredible city we’re such a big part of. Start your journey here, and get a taste of what makes our University, and our city, so special.

Watch video

A warm welcome

Voted the fourth friendliest city in the world by Rough Guides, Liverpool is a wonderfully welcoming place. We share and shape the city’s spirit – opening our doors to learners from all walks of life. This may be the first warm welcome you’ll get from Liverpool. Hopefully there’ll be many more to come.

A place to belong

We’re as diverse as the city we’re part of, with a vibrant student community drawn from across the country and around the world. On and off campus, you’ll meet people and ideas of every kind. It makes learning here a uniquely rich experience. And it shows that, when it comes to our roots, we’re as open and accessible as we’ve always been.

A pioneering spirit

When Oliver Lodge – one of our professors – made the world’s first public radio transmission from our halls in 1894, he sparked a tradition of progress and original thinking that stayed with us ever since. We remain fascinated by the big issues in the world around us. Our students and staff think freely and explore the ideas that really get them excited. Our research and teaching take each other forward every day. And whether it’s a world-changing idea or one student’s personal first, we celebrate and support progress of every kind.

The University

Original and inclusive for 140 years, we’re a truly inspirational place to learn and research. We’re also a place where students develop a rich world view, as well as the skills and confidence to thrive in today’s connected world. Although our main campus is in the heart of Liverpool, we also have a partner university in Suzhou, China. Many of our students start their studies in Liverpool, have the opportunity to spend time studying in China. And wherever you learn with us, you’ll find the same distinctive and welcoming culture.

The Original Redbrick

We opened our doors in 1881, with a mission to advance learning and ennoble life. One of the first few ‘redbrick’ universities, we were civic from the start – welcoming learners from across the city and beyond. Today, we’re an internationally renowned, globally-connected Russell Group university, associated with nine Nobel Prize winners, and home to highly-respected research and lively, original thinking. We’ve come a long way in 140 years. But in terms of what we stand for and how we do things, we haven’t changed at all.

Our city

Warm and welcoming, characterful and full of characters, there’s nowhere quite like Liverpool. It’s home to 445,000 people and one unique spirit. A Liverpool’s influence shapes the world - art, music, sport and culture, and with more galleries and museums than any other UK city apart from London The city’s entrepreneurial and creative spirit is fueling a boom in business start-ups, but Liverpool also attracts many global businesses to make the city their home. Liverpool marches to the beat of its own drum, and always has. And, like us, you’ll be proud to call it home.
